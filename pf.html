About

Avul Pakir Jinulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian
aerospace scientist and politician who served as 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
He was born on October 15,1931,in Rameswaram ,Tamil Nadu,and passed away on July 27,2015.

Contributions

Dr.Kalam played a pivot role in Indian's civilian space program
and military missile development.His vision and leadership earned him the title of
the "People's President," and he received numerous awards for his contributions to science and politics.
